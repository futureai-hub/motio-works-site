Privacy Policy

FlexZen Motio · Effective Date: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Motio Work ("we," "us," or "our") operates the FlexZen Motio mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App on Android and iOS devices.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Data (On-Device Only)

The App uses your device camera to perform real-time hand tracking using Google's MediaPipe technology. All camera processing occurs entirely on your device. We do not capture, store, record, or transmit any images, video, or camera data to any server.

2.2 Exercise Data (Local Storage)

The App stores your exercise records, routine configurations, and preferences locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data includes:

  • Exercise type, repetitions, duration, and hand mode
  • Daily routine configuration
  • App settings (landmark display, notification preferences)
  • Reminder time and weekday selections

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

2.3 Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • App open events and screen views
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Device model, OS version, and language
  • General geographic region (country-level)

This data is collected in aggregate and does not personally identify you. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

2.4 Advertising Data

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. The AdMob SDK may collect the following information:

  • Advertising ID (GAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • App interaction data for ad personalization

On iOS, the App requests your consent via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt before accessing your advertising identifier for personalized ads.

6. Advertising

The App displays the following ad formats through Google AdMob:

  • Banner Ads — displayed at the bottom of certain screens
  • Interstitial Ads — full-screen ads, limited to once per session
  • App Open Ads — shown when the app is launched or resumed, with a 30-second minimum interval

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable tracking for FlexZen Motio

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All exercise data and app settings are stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

Data collected by third-party services (Firebase Analytics, AdMob) is subject to their respective retention policies.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Data Security

We value the security of your data. The App does not operate any external servers and does not require user accounts or login. All personal exercise data remains on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
