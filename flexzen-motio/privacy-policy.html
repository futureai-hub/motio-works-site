Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 16, 2026

FlexZen Motio ("we", "our", "the App") is a daily hand movement routine app developed by Motio Works. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Camera Data: The App uses your device camera for real-time hand tracking. Camera frames are processed entirely on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

App Data: Your exercise records, daily routine settings, reminders, and preferences are all stored locally on your device and are never sent to external servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google ML Kit (MediaPipe) — for on-device hand landmark detection. All processing occurs locally.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not store or transmit camera images or video
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles
  • We do not share any data with third parties (except AdMob as described above)

4. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers or cloud storage for user data. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

  • Delete Data: You can delete all exercise records within the App's settings, or by uninstalling the App.
  • Camera Permission: You can revoke camera access at any time through your device's system settings.
  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
